OUR SERVICES

AGM construction can offer a wide range of services to fulfil requirements for a project.
Including but not limited to painting, wallpapering, electrical wiring, flooring, plumbing, joinery, cornicing and decorating.
Our tradesmen are multi skilled and professional.

Maintaining

We ensure you that unit is fit for purpose, with maximum availability at minimum costs.

Fabricating

We make something from semi-finished or raw materials rather than from ready-made components.

Site Clearance

It involves the clearing of site to allow other remedial, treatment works before the works begin.

Demolition

It involves deconstruction, taking a building apart carefully preserving elements for reuse purposes.

Piling

We do all types of piling like Driven Piles, Bored Piles, Mini Piles, and Contiguous Piling etc.

Underpinning

We repair, strengthen, or increase the depth of foundation by lowering the footing for supportive soil.
